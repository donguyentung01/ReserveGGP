Management reserves the right to change court assignments regardless of previous court requests.

Payment is due upon check-in (at the time of your reservation.) Payment is not required at the time of booking. However, if you opt to prepay for your court time, any court reservation refunds due to cancelations will be returned as an account credit (including payments made with a credit card).

In addition, please note that penalties may apply for late cancelations (4 hours or less prior to your court time).

 

Making Reservations:

  • Each individual may have no more than 1 reservation per day.

  • Reservations may be made:

    • Online on the Player Portal or via our Lifetime Activities Mobile App

    • By phone at 415-581-2540. One reservation per phone call.

  • Courts may be reserved for 30, 60 or 90 minutes at a time.

  • Verified* San Francisco residents may reserve courts 8 days in advance. Unverified Residents and Non-Residents may reserve courts 7 days in advance. Reservations open daily at 12:30pm.

*To verify your San Francisco residency, please submit this SF Resident Status Verification Form, please allow 1-2 business days for our team to update your account.

Checking In and Cancellations

  • Full payment is due at the time of check-in.

  • Reservation is forfeited after 10 minutes if court is unclaimed.

  • Fees will not be prorated for late arrivals.

  • Court assignments are subject to change.

  • Reservations may be canceled without penalty up to 4 hours before the reservation start time. Reservations can be viewed and canceled online via the Player Portal or through the Lifetime Activities Mobile App under My Reservations. You may also call us to cancel your court reservation.

  • No-shows and courts canceled less than 4 hours before start time will be charged the full court fee billable to the reservation holder.

  • During periods of inclement weather, updates on court conditions will be made available via push notifications on the Lifetime Activities Mobile App. You may also call the front desk at 415-581-2540 for an update on court conditions.

Fees & Rate Policies

  • Fees are per court, not per person.

  • Resident & Non-resident fees – If there is 1 resident, then the resident fee applies. Fees are assessed based on the person making the reservation.

  • Senior Discount – If there is one senior, then the senior fee applies. Fees are assessed based on the person making the reservation.

  • Youth Free Play – all players must be under the age of 18 for free use of the courts during the designated time frame.

  • Account credit will be issued for unused time interrupted by inclement weather.

Rain Procedure

On rainy days, staff may close the courts until they are playable. Call 415-581-2540 to see if courts are dry.

Credit will be issued for paid, unused time if it rains during play. Make ups will be scheduled for lesson and league rainouts.

Court Rules & Etiquette

Please follow these rules to ensure safety and enjoyment for all 

  • Players must follow all current Covid-19 safety guidelines

  • Non-marking shoes and shirts required (no open toed shoes)

  • Keep noise to an appropriate level and be courteous and respectful of other players and staff

  • No unauthorized tennis instruction allowed

  • No personal ball machines or ball hoppers

  • Please pick up all tennis balls & dispose trash in the appropriate bins

  • Wait until point in play is complete before retrieving a ball from another court

  • Be safe. Before starting play, ensure the court is free from hazards (extra balls, cans or other debris)

  • No smoking or alcoholic beverages

  • No chewing gum, or open food/drink containers on court

  • No biking, skating, or skateboarding on court 

  • No unattended children that are not playing tennis/pickleball/table tennis

  • No pets on court

Pet Policy

As a courtesy to all visitors of the Lisa & Douglas Goldman Tennis Center, please adhere to the following guidelines: 

  • Service and support animals must be on-leash (6ft leash or shorter) and under the owner’s control at all times or in a carrier. 

  • Service and support animals are permitted on court provided that they are in a carrier or remain on leash and tied to the interior of the court and in proximity of the owner who is playing. 

  • Service and support animals may not be left unattended or have leash tied in a walkway or common area. 

  • Service and support animals must be well-behaved. Those exhibiting unsafe or disruptive behavior will be asked to leave. 

  • Service and support animals must not be on furniture and may not be fed or watered indoors. 

  • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their service and support animals.
